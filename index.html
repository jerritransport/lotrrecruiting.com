Life on the Road Recruiting & Transportation Services is a registered C/TPA and transportation workforce partner serving motor carriers, CDL schools, airlines, and owner-operators across the country.

We manage drug and alcohol testing programs, FMCSA Clearinghouse reporting, random testing pools, and driver qualification files — so carriers of all sizes stay compliant without the administrative burden.

Second-Chance CDL Recruiting

DOL Apprenticeship Programs

What Is a DOL Registered Apprenticeship?

A U.S. Department of Labor Registered Apprenticeship is a structured, employer-driven training program that combines on-the-job learning with related technical instruction — leading to a nationally recognized credential.

How LOTR Can Help Your Company

We work with transportation employers to assess apprenticeship readiness, connect them with the TITAAN network and DJH Express Academy, and support the integration of a DOL-registered program into their existing workforce development model.

Who This Is For

Any transportation employer — trucking companies, logistics providers, transit agencies, aviation employers — looking to build a structured pipeline of trained, credentialed workers and reduce long-term turnover.
